Est. 2009 · Harare

An estate-management firm built on quiet competence.

Scapes & Décor is the kind of property services team estate managers keep on retainer for years — not the loudest name in the market, the most reliable one on site.

Who we are

Practitioners, not marketers.

Scapes & Décor (Pvt) Ltd was founded in Harare in 2009 to bring landscaping, property maintenance, painting & finishing, solar lighting and project delivery under a single accountable team. Since then, we've worked across residential estates, commercial premises and institutional grounds — always with the same small-firm attention that led clients to choose us in the first place.

"We'd rather be the firm your estate manager trusts implicitly than the loudest name in the market."
Our Vision

To be Zimbabwe's most trusted name in integrated property services — setting the standard for maintained, well-presented environments.

Our Mission

To deliver grounded, accountable property services that protect asset value and elevate everyday experience.

What we believe

Five principles we organise around.

  • Show up on schedule.Retainer clients receive a published attendance plan. Predictability is the foundation of trust.
  • One point of accountability.A named project lead owns the relationship end-to-end. No contractor juggling, no finger-pointing.
  • Quote what we deliver.Itemised proposals with clear inclusions — so you know exactly what you're paying for before work begins.
  • Direct employees, not day labour.Trained, uniformed crews who know your property, its quirks, and your preferred standard of finish.
  • Work with the climate, not against it.Plant selection, paint systems and irrigation schemes suited to the Highveld — not imported defaults that struggle here.

The team

A small, direct team — deliberately.

Our crews are direct employees, not subcontractors sourced by the job. That means the same faces return to your property month after month, the same hands install your planting and prune it six months later, the same painter finishes the façade and touches up the next season. Consistency is how standards hold.

For multi-discipline projects, a dedicated project lead coordinates across divisions so you have one person to call — not five.

Ready to work with a team that stays?

Tell us about the property and we'll arrange a walk-through within the week — no obligation, no pressure.
