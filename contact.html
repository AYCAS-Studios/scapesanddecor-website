Before you write

A few things that speed us up.

What information is useful in a first message?

Property type and location, which service(s) you're considering, and a rough sense of scope or timeline. Photos are hugely helpful for painting and maintenance — attach them if you have any.

How quickly can you arrange a site visit?

Within the week for Harare. For properties outside Harare we'll confirm travel arrangements when we reply. Site visits are free for genuine enquiries.

Do you quote over the phone?

Only for very small, well-defined jobs. Anything involving grounds, multi-room painting, or multi-discipline works gets an on-site assessment first — ballpark pricing without eyes on the property tends to mislead everyone.

Do you take on emergency / after-hours call-outs?

Retainer clients have an after-hours number. For non-retainer clients we prioritise during business hours. If it's genuinely urgent, WhatsApp us — we usually see it.
