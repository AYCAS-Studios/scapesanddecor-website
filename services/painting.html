Overview

Painting, done the patient way.

A paint job is only as good as what happens before the first brush stroke. We spend as much time on preparation — sanding, filling, cleaning, priming, remediating damp — as we do on the finish coats themselves. That is what separates a paint job that still looks right in three years from one that flakes off by the first rainy season.

We handle full-property exteriors, room-by-room interiors, timber and metal finishing, decorative finishes, waterproofing coatings and new-build preparation. Whether it's a single feature wall, a full facade repaint or preparing a rental for tenant turnover, the process is the same.

We are specific about paint selection — we stick with reputable local and imported brands matched to the substrate, exposure and expected wear. Cheap paint is almost never cheaper in the long run, and we will tell you that plainly at quotation stage.

FAQs

The questions we hear most.

Which paint brands do you work with?

Our default specification draws from Dulux, Plascon, Astra and Dura — brands with consistent supply, proven performance in the Zimbabwean climate and genuine technical back-up. We'll match products to substrate and exposure, and we're happy to work with a client-specified brand provided it is fit-for-purpose.

Can you paint during the rainy season?

Interior work runs year-round. For exteriors we plan around the rains — dry-season painting is always preferred. If a job has to run through a wet month, we schedule around weather windows, monitor humidity and sequence coats to respect the manufacturer's minimum dry times. We will not rush a finish coat onto a damp substrate.

Do you offer colour consultation?

Yes, as part of every significant project. We bring physical swatches to site, paint large test patches on your actual walls (daylight differs from paper cards), and talk through how a colour will age, weather and sit against your finishes. No extra charge on scoped jobs.

What warranty do you offer on painting work?

Workmanship warranty of 12 months on interior repaints and 24 months on full-system exterior repaints, provided the specified paint system is used. Manufacturer warranties on the paint itself pass through directly. If the paint flakes, bubbles or fails adhesion within the warranty window for reasons attributable to us, we return and remediate at no charge.
