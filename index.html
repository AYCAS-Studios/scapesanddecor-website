About the Firm

An estate-management firm built on quiet competence.

Scapes & Décor (Pvt) Ltd is a Zimbabwean property services company serving residential estates, commercial premises and institutional clients. We are practitioners first — the kind of team property owners keep on retainer for years.

"We'd rather be the firm your estate manager trusts implicitly than the loudest name in the market."
Our Vision

To be Zimbabwe's most trusted name in integrated property services — setting the standard for maintained, well-presented environments.

Our Mission

To deliver grounded, accountable property services that protect asset value and elevate everyday experience.

Our Approach

A five-step process, refined on site.

Every engagement — whether a single paint job or a multi-year grounds retainer — follows the same disciplined sequence. No surprises, no drift.

01—

Consult

On-site walk-through to understand property, priorities and standards expected.

02—

Assess

Condition report, scope definition, photography and measurable baselines.

03—

Plan

Costed proposal, schedule and team allocation — signed off before any work begins.

04—

Execute

Trained crews, scheduled attendance, supervision and progress updates throughout.

05—

Maintain

Handover, snag-list close-out and — where appropriate — ongoing care retainer.
